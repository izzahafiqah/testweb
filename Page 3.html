(How To Train Your Dragon)

To give you a basic rundown of the plot, it starts with a young Viking named Hiccup who is determined to follow his father's legacy and fight the dragons that raid their village. When Hiccup eventually strikes a dragon with the catapult and discovers it crash-landed in the forest, unable to bring himself to kill it. Hiccup quickly realizes that dragons are not at all what the Vikings have always imagined. Hiccup and the dragon, whom he dubs Toothless, eventually form a connection that will broaden both of their horizons as the obsevant boy discovers that his people have underestimated the species. However, when the two split off in their different ways, they discover that they must battle the harmful ignorance that plagues their world.

(How To Train Your Dragon 2)

It's been five years since Hiccup and Toothless reunited the dragons and Viking of Berk. While Astrid, Snotlout, and the rest of the gang compete in dragon races (The island's new favourite contact sport), the now-inseparable duo navigates the island's uncharted regions. During one of their escapades, they come across a hidden ice cave full of wild dragons and a mysterious dragon rider who turns out to be Hiccup's long-lost mother, Valka. Hiccup and Toothless are caught in the middle of a battle to protect Berk from a power-hungry warrior named Drago. Now, the two must band together to fight for what they believe in, knowing that only by working together can they influence the destiny of both mankind and dragons.

(How To Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World)

The Vikings continue to rehabilitate captured dragons in order to bring them to Berk, a thriving dragon and human utopia. As a consequence of their efforts, the island has become overrun with dragons. Hiccup’s ambition to uncover the “Hidden World”, a safe place for dragons mentioned by his late father Stoick, in a response to the overcrowding. Meanwhile, Toothless meets his ideal date in the form of this Light Fury, who was kidnapped by warlords and delivered to renown dragon hunter, Grimmel the Grisly (Night Fury Killer) as bait for him to catch Toothless for use as an alpha by the warlords. The Light Fury, on the other hand, falls in love with Toothless (Night Fury) and teaches him how to control his abilities. After Hiccup and his companions defeated Grimmel in a fight at the end of the film, Hiccup made a tough decision to letting go his buddy to the secret realm, ensuring the dragons’ safety.
